Beef Brisket Recipe

Description

This is a delicious easy to make brisket recipe. Perfect for the holidays!

Ingredients

  • 1 (3 pound) beef brisket, trimmed of fat
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 (12oz)can beer
  • 1 (12oz) bottle tomato-based chili sauce
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Preheat the oven to 325F
  3. Season brisket on all sides with salt and pepper and place in a glass baking dish. Cover with a layer of sliced onion
  4. Mix together beer,chili sauce, and brown sugar in a medium bowl. Pour over the roast. Cover the dish tightly with aluminum foil.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 3 hours. Remove the foil and bake for an additional three minutes
  6. Let brisket reset and cool slightly before slicing and returning to the dish. Reheat in the oven spooned over the sliced meat.
