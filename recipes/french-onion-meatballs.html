French Onion Recipe

Description

French onion chicken meatballs are richly seasoned meatballs baked with a rich French onion sauce and topped with melted Gruyère cheese. This meal is fit for hosting guests. Serve them with egg noodles or seasoned red potatoes and French bread on the side.

Ingredients

  • 1 pund ground chicken
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 package dry French onion soup
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 2 medium onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/3 cup red wine
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 6 oz Gruyère cheese, shredded

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Combine ground chicken, bread crumbs, onion soup mix, egg, and garlic in a medium bowl until well incorporated; form into sixteen 1 1/2-inch meatballs.
  3. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Add Onions and sugar; cook until onions are softened, about 12 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, heat olive oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Add meatballs; cook, flipping occasionally, until edges are browned, about 4 minutes.
  5. Stir red wine, salt, and black pepper into onions; cook over medium heat for 3 minutes. Stir in beef broth and thyme; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low; slowly stir in flour. Cook until slightly thickened, 2 to 3 minutes more
  6. Remove meatballs from the oven; pour onion sauce over top. Cover with cheese; return skilled to the oven until melted, about 5 minutes.
